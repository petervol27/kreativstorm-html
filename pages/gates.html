Gates of fire is a book written as a story told by a refugee of the spartan forces to the emperor of persia, the book tells the story of a slave captured and brought to sparta who grows up there learns the customs and lives his life alongside the spartans, at the battle of thermopylae he is captured by the persians and tells the story of the greatest warriors to ever live, a king who ruled by example and went to knock on death's door for the sake of his people, trully a story that brings everything beautiful about the spartans and the battle of the hot gates, this book is a total recomendation for anyone.
